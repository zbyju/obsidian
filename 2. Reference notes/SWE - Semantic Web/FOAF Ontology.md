It is used for describing:
- People
- their activities
- their relations to other people and objects

It can be used for building a homepage of a person and describing himself.

It offers, for example:
- foaf:Person
- foaf:Group
- foaf:name
- foaf:homepage
- foaf:knows
- foaf:mbox
- foaf:depiction
